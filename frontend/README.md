# 🏔️ The Himalayans - Hotel & Homestay Booking Platform

HimStay connects travelers with verified hotels and homestays across the beautiful Himalayan region of Uttarakhand. We make finding and booking your next mountain getaway simple, fast, and reliable.

### 🏨 For Hotel & Homestay Owners
* **List Your Property:** Easily register your space on our platform to reach more travelers.
* **Manage Bookings:** Get direct access to incoming booking requests through our dedicated dashboard.
* **Verified Stays:** Join a platform dedicated to quality stays, helping you grow your local tourism business.

### 🗺️ For Travelers
* **Discover Stays:** Browse verified accommodations in Rishikesh, Uttarkashi, Matli, and across the Himalayas.
* **Easy Booking:** Simple and reliable booking process with real-time status updates on your trips.
* **Direct Support:** Get in touch with property owners instantly via WhatsApp for any queries.
